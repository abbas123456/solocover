What is Solocover?

Solcover is a song cover upload site and was created to combat the constant barrage of talent shows polluting our screens today by giving ordinary people a portal to explore other people's potential as well as their own.

How it works

The site is centred around something called threads. A thread is a page that lists a collection of cover songs performed by the users of the site. The thread also has a spotify play button that allows you to listen to the original track if you have spotify installed. Comments can be added to the thread and if you are logged in you can cast a vote for your favourite performances.

What you can do

If you fancy yourself as a bit of singer then please upload your songs, however, songs cannot be longer than a minute. If you don't want to sing you are still free to create a profile and listen to, comment on and vote for other user's songs.

Want more features?

Please email us if you think the site can do with more features or if you just want to give general feedback

Can you code?

This site is completely open source, the code can be found on github. Feel free to fork the project and add whatever features you like.
